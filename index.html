09 / FAQ

Quick answers
before we talk.

What do you need from me to get started?

A detailed brief or a quick meeting call. I need to understand your goals, needs, audience, brand style, the purpose of the website and some other details I will need. That's it — I handle the rest.

What if I'm not sure what style or layout I want?

No problem — that's actually common. I can guide you with examples, ideas and directions until we find the style that fits your brand.

Can I contact you after you have completed my site?

Yes, that's important for me also. You can contact me whenever you need help with anything — fixes, updates, advice, or new ideas. I stay reachable long after launch.

How long does a project usually take?

It depends on scope. Landing pages are typically 2 weeks, full custom websites 3–6 weeks, and bigger builds 4–8 weeks. You get a clear timeline with milestones before we start — no vague promises.

Will I be able to update the site myself?

Yes. I build everything so you stay in control — clean CMS setups, clear documentation, and a short walkthrough so you can update text, images and products without needing a developer for every small change.

Do you handle hosting, domain and email?

I can. I help you pick the right hosting, connect your domain, set up professional email and make sure everything is configured for speed, security and SEO before launch.

What if I'm not happy with the result?

I work in clear stages with feedback at each milestone, so issues never pile up. If something isn't right, I revise it until it is — and there's a walk-away guarantee on the first milestone, so your risk is zero.
